Property Tax Information & Resources

Understanding Your Property Tax

Property taxes fund essential services such as schools, fire protection, and other local government services. Your tax bill is calculated based on:

  • Your property's assessed value
  • The levy rates for each taxing district where your property is located
  • Any applicable exemptions or special programs
