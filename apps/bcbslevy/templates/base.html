To calculate a property tax levy, navigate to Levy Calculator from the main menu. Select your tax district, enter the target year, and provide assessed value information. The system will calculate the levy rate and amount based on your inputs.

You can also create different scenarios to compare outcomes.

To import property data, go to Data Management → Import Data. You can upload files in various formats including XLS, XLSX, CSV, and TXT.

The system will guide you through mapping columns and validation before finalizing the import.

LevyMaster offers several types of reports:

  • Tax District Summaries - Overview of districts and their levy rates
  • Property Reports - Detailed property information and tax history
  • Historical Analysis - Multi-year trend analysis of tax rates
  • Compliance Reports - Regulatory compliance verification
  • Forecast Reports - Projected future levy rates and amounts

All reports can be exported in multiple formats including PDF, Excel, and CSV.
