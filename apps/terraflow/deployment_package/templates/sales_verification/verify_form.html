Washington Sales Qualification

Per Washington Department of Revenue guidelines, sales may be disqualified if:

  • Sale is between relatives or corporate affiliates
  • Sale involves government agencies or public utilities
  • Sale includes trades, partial interests, or estate liquidation
  • Deed type is not typical of market sales
  • Sale includes significant personal property
  • Sale involves distress, auction, or forced conditions
